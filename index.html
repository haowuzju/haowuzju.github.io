The Bride and Groom

Xingyun (廖行韵)

Xingyun (Lizzy) is a girl who likes to laugh. Born in Changsha (长沙), China, she is a tall girl among peers. After receiving her Bachelor degree from Zhejiang University, Lizzy enjoyed the rural life in the Midwest, attending University of Michigan at Ann Arbor, and lived in the corn fields in Indiana for several months. After two years of study, Lizzy moved to Boston with a Master's degree. During most of her time in Boston, she played Sanguosha (三国杀) with her friends. Every now and then when she needs some rest from the game, she works as an Actuary. Now she is looking forward to her new life in the Golden State. (Exciting!)

Hao (吴昊)

Hao is a handsome boy by all means. Born and raised in Henan (河南), China, Hao has wanted to become a scientist ever since his childhood, because for some unknown reasons, he thought Scientist is a really cool profession. Hao majored in playing Basketball up to college, getting to know some of his best friends in life. After his college life in Zhejiang University, Hao came to the sunshining Southern California and successfully transitioned to the field of Electrical Engineering. Now that he has obtained his Doctorate degree from UCLA (Go Bruins!), he can proudly say that he has fullfilled his childhood dream - "I'm a scientist!".

OUR STORY

Lizzy and Hao both feel fortunate to call the beautiful city Hangzhou as the place they met each other. They firstly met in their freshmen year at Zhejiang University, outside the interview classroom of Melton Foundation. At the time none of them realized that the other person would become the most important one in their lives. It took them a year to recognize that they both liked each other, then naturally, they fell in love. They spent the following fantastic two years at Hangzhou, then five and half years of long-distance love between east and west coasts. They missed each other every single day, went for some amazing adventures, now they are ready to spend the rest of their lives together.

ABOUT Descanso Garden

Descanso Gardens is an urban retreat of year-round natural beauty, internationally renowned botanical collections and spectacular seasonal horticultural displays. In 1953, Descanso founder E. Manchester Boddy preserved these 160 acres of gardens, woodlands and chaparral for future generations to experience the natural heritage and beauty of Southern California. Accredited by the American Association of Museums, Descanso Gardens welcomes thousands of visitors annually, from local members to international travelers.    Go here for more information.

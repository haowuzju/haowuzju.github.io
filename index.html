The Bride and Groom

Xingyun (廖行韵)

Xingyun (Lizzy) is a girl who likes to laugh. Born in Changsha, the entertainment hub of China, she never lacks a sense of humor and always enjoys entertaining others. After receiving her Bachelor Degree from Zhejiang University, Lizzy enjoyed two years of peaceful life in the beautiful Ann Arbor, Michigan for graduate school, and then moved to Boston. During most of her time in Boston, she played Sanguosha (三国杀) with her friends. Every now and then when she needs some rest from the game, she works as an actuary, wearing suits and talk with numbers. Now she is looking forward to her new life in the Golden State California. (Exciting!)

Hao (吴昊)

Hao is a handsome boy from the historical city Luoyang, China. For some unknown reasons, he thought Scientist is a really cool profession and always wanted to become a scientist ever since childhood. Hao played basketball a lot up to college, getting to know some of his best lifetime friends. After his college life in Zhejiang University, Hao came to the sunshining Southern California and successfully transitioned to the field of Electrical Engineering. Now with his Doctorate degree from UCLA (Go Bruins!), he can proudly say that he has fulfilled his childhood dream - "I'm a scientist!".

OUR STORY

Lizzy and Hao firstly met each other in the beautiful city Hangzhou, in their freshmen year at Zhejiang University. Outside the interview classroom of Melton Foundation, Hao made a self introduction to Lizzy and they started to chat. At the time none of them realized that the other person would become the most important one in their lives. It took them a year to recognize that they both liked each other, then naturally, they fell in love. They spent the following two fantastic years at Hangzhou, then six years of long-distance love between east and west coasts in United States. They missed each other every single day, went for some amazing adventures, now they are ready to spend the rest of their lives together.

ABOUT Descanso Garden

Descanso Gardens is an urban retreat of year-round natural beauty, internationally renowned botanical collections and spectacular seasonal horticultural displays. In 1953, Descanso founder E. Manchester Boddy preserved these 160 acres of gardens, woodlands and chaparral for future generations to experience the natural heritage and beauty of Southern California. Accredited by the American Association of Museums, Descanso Gardens welcomes thousands of visitors annually, from local members to international travelers.    Go here for more information.
